About

I'm Kory and I'm currently a 4th year Computer Science major at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I am from Cochran, Georgia and I have been interested in computers ever since I was young. When I began coding in high school, I realized it was what I wanted to do in life. I would describe myself as a hard-worker, a fast learner, and a independent thinker that can also work well on a team. My work ethic is often commented on as "annoying" but I just strongly believe in giving each project my all.
